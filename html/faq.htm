How do I place an order?

Ordering at SAINI is easy. Placing your first order at SAINI? Just select the items you want to shop, enter your shipping address and payment information and you are home. If you need any assistance give us a call. We would love to take your order over phone.

  • A user friendly interface
  • Find out and sort suitable partners.
  • Free registration and online customer support.
  • Best-in-class match-making services.

How will my order be delivered to me?

Your order would be delivered through reputed courier companies at your doorstep.

Do you take orders over phone?

Yes. We do take orders over phone. The payment mode possible for these orders is Cash On Delivery only.

How do I check the status of my order?

Your order status is updated to you via emails and sms at every step. Once your order is placed you would receive an sms and an email with your order details. Again after your order is dispatched we send you an sms with your tracking details. Please check your spam and old sms-es for the status of your order, if you don't see any updates. In case of any unforeseen events which delays your order you would receive a special update from our end.

What are the payment options?

The best way to transfer funds is via Paypal. This secure platform ensures timely payments and a secure environment.

When do I get paid?

Our standard payment plan provides for monthly payments. At the end of each month, all accumulated funds are transfered to your account. The minimum amount of your balance should be at least 70 USD.
